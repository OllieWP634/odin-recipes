Full English Breakfast

Home Page

Description

A full English breakfast consists of a variety of foods, aimed to stack flavours in unique manners. This meal was prepared by british farmers to fulfill an appetite, required for a full day of work.

Serves: 1 | Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 white bread slices
  • 2 sausages
  • 2 back bacon rashers
  • 2 hashbrowns
  • 1 egg
  • 1 black pudding
  • 20g butter

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 200Â°C, add hashbrowns once heated. Cook these for 10 minutes
  2. Add oil to pan, on medium heat add sausages, cook for 5 minutes
  3. Add bacon to pan, cook for a further 5 minutes with the sausages
  4. 10 minutes have passed, flip the hash browns and add black pudding to air fryer
  5. Turn heat to low, crack the egg on a flat surface and add to pan
  6. Cook egg for 3 minutes, then plate everything from the pan
  7. Add butter to pan, fry white bread on low heat for 5 minutes
  8. Plate bread, hashbrowns and black pudding
