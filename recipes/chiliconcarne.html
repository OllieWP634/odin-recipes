Chili Con Carne

Home Page

Description

Chili con carne is a spicy stew of mexican origin.

Serves: 4 | Cooking Time: 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 500g 20% fat ground beef
  • 1 brown onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 30g tomato puree
  • 400g red kidney beans
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 rich beef stock cube
  • 15g dark chocolate
  • 400g basmati rice (dried)

Steps

  1. Dice onion, red pepper and garlic cloves
  2. Add oil to pan, then cook onion and garlic on medium heat
  3. Add 500g of ground beef to pan, break down the mince using a spatula, cook until browned
  4. Add 400g chopped tomatoes, 30g tomato puree and 1 rich beef stock cube to pan alongside 400ml water
  5. Add 15g dark chocolate, 400g red kidney beans and red pepper. Then simmer on a low heat until thick
  6. Cook 400g basmati rice while chili simmers
