Steak and Eggs

Home Page

Description

Steak and eggs, a dish consisting of beefsteak and fried eggs, originated within Australian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Steak (cut of choice)
  • Free range egg
  • Spring onion
  • Cooking oil
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Leave the steak out for an hour before cooking, season with paprika, salt and pepper.
  2. Chop spring onions and leave to the side.
  3. Ensure pan is incredibly hot, add oil of choice, then begin seering the steak. Do not turn over until a crust has been developed.
  4. Flip the steak over, add spring onions to the pan, then add butter in order to baste the steak.
  5. Remove the steak from the pan and allow to rest for 5 minutes.
  6. Crack an egg on a flat surface, add to the pan and allow it to cook while the steak rests.
  7. Once the egg has been cooked, chop and plate the steak and eggs!
